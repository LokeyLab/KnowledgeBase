# Solid-Phase Synthesis

Protocols for peptide assembly on solid support. The Lokey Lab primarily uses Fmoc/tBu chemistry on 2-chlorotrityl resin, with synthesis performed on the Prelude peptide synthesizer or manually in fritted synthesis vessels.

## Protocols

- [Coupling Reactions](coupling-reactions.md) —Overview of amide, ester, and peptoid coupling methods.
- [N-Methylation](n-methylation.md) —Fmoc N-methylation via the oxazolidinone route.
- [In-Sequence Methylation](in-sequence-methylation.md) —On-resin methylation of the terminal residue using Mitsunobu chemistry.
- [Sidechain Tyrosine Resin Loading](sidechain-tyrosine-resin-loading.md) —Loading Fmoc-O-Allyl-Tyrosine through the sidechain for on-resin cyclization.
- [Esterification](esterification.md) —Solid-phase ester bond formation for depsipeptide synthesis.
- [Peptoid Coupling](peptoid-coupling.md) —Bromoacetic acid/DIC method for peptoid residue incorporation.
- [BTC Coupling](btc-coupling.md) —Triphosgene-mediated coupling for difficult amide bonds.
