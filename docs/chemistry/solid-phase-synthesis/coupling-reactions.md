# Coupling Reactions

## Amide

Peptide bond formation on solid phase and in solution.

### Solid Phase

- **Uronium-Based Coupling** -- HATU, HBTU, COMU, and related uronium/guanidinium reagents.
- **Phosphonium Reagents** -- PyBOP, PyAOP, and related phosphonium coupling agents.
- **Carbodiimide** -- DIC-mediated coupling, typically with HOAt or HOBt as additive.
- **[BTC](btc-coupling.md)** -- Triphosgene-mediated coupling for sterically hindered substrates.

### Solution Phase

- **DCC** -- Dicyclohexylcarbodiimide coupling.
- **EDC** -- Water-soluble carbodiimide for aqueous-compatible couplings.
- **CDI** -- Carbonyldiimidazole-mediated activation.
- **[Solution-Phase Amide Coupling with DSC](../solution-phase/amide-coupling-dsc.md)** -- Disuccinimidyl carbonate activation.
- **Active Esters** -- Pre-formed active esters (e.g., NHS esters, pentafluorophenyl esters).

### Other Reagents to Consider

- Acid fluoride (TFFH, cyanuric fluoride)
- Acid chloride (BTC, trichloroacetonitrile, thionyl chloride)
- T3P
- EEDQ and IIDQ
- Triazines
- PyBrOP
