Voodoo Free Chemistry

Mission Statements:

  • This is meant to be a place to record and share our most successful protocols.
  • Free from the bureaucracy of SOP's, this is meant to be an editable record so that our lab can evolve at the rate of each of its members.
  • Too long have our methods been confined to lab-notebooks and oral histories.
  • Make sure things are codified here for the ages and make this a resource for our lab and those in it.
